Overview

A personal management app designed for professionals and students. It provides a minimalistic and easy-to-use framework for goal setting, project/task management, and achieving balance in life. Flourish is made by LucentSystems, a brand dedicated to integrating nature with technology solutions.

Capture

The Capture feature allows you to freeform write and capture your thoughts, helping to alleviate the burden on your mind.

Organize

Organize your thoughts and derive tasks, goals, and projects from them. Create a structured framework to manage your priorities.

Plan

Plan your goals and prioritize them based on their importance. Gain clarity and create a roadmap to achieve them.

Focus

The Focus feature provides a Pomodoro timer and ambiance to help you concentrate on a selected task. It assists in task selection and enhances your focus during work sessions.
